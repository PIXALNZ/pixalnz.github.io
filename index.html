Ngā Kupu

Pēhea te Tākaro | How to Play

Guess the KUPU in 6 tries.

Each guess must be a valid 5-letter te reo Māori word.

The tile colors show how close your guess was:

W H A R E

W is in the word and in the correct spot.

M A O R I

A is in the word but in the wrong spot.

K U P U A

The second U is not in the word.

Kia kaha! A new word is available after each game.
